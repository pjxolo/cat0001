Overview

There are 788 nodes on energy.gov assigned to CESER. Since January 2023, these nodes have received 769,354 pageviews.

Key Findings

  • 20% of all nodes have not been updated this decade
  • 70% of all pageviews since 1/1/2023 were on the top 25 pages

Recomendations

  2. Archive old press releases (pre 2020)
  3. Review top pages for quality assurance and SEO.
  4. Segment the SPR content for future analysis/migration
  5. Review the top landing pages to prep for redirects and migration planning
  6. Prep top 25 pages for migration to new content model, site structure.
